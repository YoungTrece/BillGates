Bill Gates is still the richest man in the world, Forbes found in its annual listing of billionaires. Gates, with a net worth of $75 billion, has held the top spot for three years. He's followed on this year's list by the Spanish businessman Amancio Ortega ($67 billion) and investor Warren Buffett ($60.8 billion).
