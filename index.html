Microsoft Acquisitions:

| Skype

| Nokia

| LinkedIn

Microsoft bought Skype in May 2011 for $8.5 billion.

Microsoft's 2013 acquisition of Nokia was really a desperation move. Nokia was reportedly planning to abandon Windows Phone for Android. Microsoft figured it was worth $7.2 billion to keep the Windows Phone experiment alive

Microsoft bought LinkedIn for $26.2 billion. Microsoft paid $196 per share for the company. LinkedIn CEO Jeff Weiner remained CEO of the social network for professionals.
